Difference between Margin, Border, and Padding.

The border is the space directly around your content that you have added to your HTML page. You can colorthe border visible if you choose, but the what ever you do to it the content will reman insided the border.

padding is the space between the content and the border.

Margin is the space between content borders. The bigger the margin the bigger the space in between the content and vic versa if you have no margin the content will be dirictly on top of each other.
